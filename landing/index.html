Everything you need to work remotely

One app. See the screen, operate it by touch, or use it as a precision remote.

🖥️

Live screen streaming

H.264 video of your Mac, full-screen in landscape. Tap what you see to click it.

👆

Tap, drag, scroll, zoom

Tap to click, hold-drag to move files, two-finger scroll, pinch to zoom, long-press to right-click.

⌨️

Keyboard & trackpad

A real trackpad with predicted touch, plus a pop-up keyboard that types straight to your Mac.

🎛️

Action deck

One-tap tiles: launch apps, play/pause, volume, brightness, lock, run Shortcuts.

🌍

Works anywhere

Same WiFi or across the internet — it switches paths automatically. Leave your Mac, work from anywhere.

🔒

End-to-end encrypted

Noise IK + ChaCha20-Poly1305. The relay only ever sees ciphertext — never your screen or keystrokes.

Up and running in a minute

Pair once, then it just reconnects.

Install the helper on your Mac

A tiny menu-bar app. Grant Accessibility (control) and Screen Recording (view) once.

Scan the QR with your iPhone

A time-limited, single-use code pairs the two over an authenticated, encrypted channel.

See & control your Mac

Open the Screen tab, rotate to landscape, and operate your Mac by touch — apps, docs, email.

Leave — and keep working

Off WiFi, it reconnects through the relay automatically. No VPN, no port-forwarding.

Zero-knowledge by design

Your Mac's screen and every keystroke are end-to-end encrypted between your devices. The relay that connects you when you're away blind-forwards ciphertext and can never read it.
